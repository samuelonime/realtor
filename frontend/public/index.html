Core Modules

Everything your team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Six tightly integrated modules that cover the entire real estate sales cycle — from the first lead to the final payment.

🎯

Lead Management

Capture, assign, and nurture leads through every stage of your sales funnel.

  • Multi-source lead capture
  • Bulk agent assignment
  • Timestamped notes & follow-ups
  • Status pipeline tracking
🏢

Property Listings

Manage your entire property portfolio with images, details, and agent assignments.

  • Multiple listing images
  • Filter by type, price & status
  • Direct link to deals
  • Agent property views
🤝

Deal Pipeline

Open and track deals tied to your leads and properties with full audit trails.

  • Deal value & commission tracking
  • Stage-by-stage updates
  • Per-agent deal views
  • Full change history
💰

Payment Tracking

Record installments, track balances, and know who received what — in real time.

  • Payment against any deal
  • Installment & milestone tracking
  • Outstanding balance view
  • Full payment audit log
📄

Document Hub

Upload contracts, certificates, and property documents — all organized and secured.

  • Property-linked file uploads
  • Document type categories
  • Uploader & date tracking
  • Team-wide access (role-gated)
📊

Live Dashboard

A real-time command center showing your entire business at a glance.

  • Lead & deal KPIs
  • Revenue metrics (MTD)
  • Conversion rates
  • Role-filtered data views
